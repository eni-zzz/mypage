Please read before trading or buying!

When trading with me:

  1. Please send me a video of your card with the flash on and a screenshot of the card you want to trade it for.I will of course do the same.
  2. If we both agree to trade we should make it clear of when we are going to pack and send the card.
  3. We should always send each other a picture of the card packaged with the adress visible to confirm it's the right item and the adress is written correctly.
  4. After confirming all the above comes the sending off. Please always send a video or else there is just no valid proof.
  5. Additional note: I always use toploader, if you aren't using any please tell me how you are going to package so I can judge if it's safe enough to my standard.

When buying from me:

  1. Please send me a screenshot of the post with the card you are interested in circled.
  2. If the card is available I will send you a video of the card with the flash on so you can see if it's in good condition.If I notice anything I will of course tell you.
  3. I will then share my paypal link or etransfer e-mail so you can pay. Please note that I only accept usd through f&f for paypal.
  4. I always send the packaging and sending proof.
  5. When you receive your card I always appreciate when people dm me or tag me in their story saying it arrives so i can add it to my proof highlight.
